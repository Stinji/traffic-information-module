Traffic Information Module

How It Works

This module will help you practice giving traffic information using the standard format:

Traffic, [clock position], [distance], [direction], [level], [other information]

  1. 1

    You’ll be presented with a radar scenario showing aircraft positions and trajectories.

  2. 2

    Analyse the situation and say the traffic information out loud.

  3. 3

    Check your answer and rate your performance as you work through all scenarios.
